About Us

Learn about our mission, vision and background.

Mission

To make housing information and assistance easier to access for Jamaicans by providing reliable guidance, affordable service options and supportive customer care.

Vision

To become a trusted community housing agency that helps more families move toward safe, secure and affordable home ownership.

Background

Edward’s Housing Agency was created to support people who need help understanding housing schemes, financing, application requirements and contributor benefits. The agency focuses on community relevance, simple communication and practical assistance.
