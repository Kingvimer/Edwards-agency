Housing Services

Three main housing-related services offered by Edward’s Housing Agency.

Home Ownership Schemes

Affordable houses and lots are offered in planned communities for qualified applicants.

  • Lower-cost housing units
  • Priority for first-time homeowners
  • Guidance on eligibility and application steps

Housing Loans & Financing

Loan information helps citizens understand mortgages, repayment terms and combined financing.

  • Long-term mortgages
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Support for income-based planning

Contributor Assistance

Contributors may qualify for reduced interest rates, grants or other housing support.

  • Reduced loan rates
  • Grants and subsidies
  • Home improvement assistance
